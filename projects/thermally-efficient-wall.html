Thermally Efficient Wall

Problem

CSU Sacramento needed alternative thermally efficient designs for a south-facing wall of a new building.

Solution

Created a control wall per 2022 Building Energy Efficiency Standards. Used NSRDB weather data (30-minute intervals) to simulate a year of heat transfer for four designs, then compared thermal performance and cost to determine the optimal design.

Tools Used

MATLABNSRDB weather data2022 Building Energy Efficiency Standards

Highlights

  • Year-long heat transfer simulation
  • Compared coldest/hottest day performance and annual cost
