Blood Culture Volume Gauge

Problem

Blood culture bottles from various collection sites must be filled to correct volumes. Previously, staff marked three separate bottles by hand (wasteful and time-consuming).

Solution

Designed a single rotatable gauge for aerobic, anaerobic, and pediatric bottles. Align the gauge to the bottle type and confirm fill volume by checking whether it falls within a designated “blue zone.” Reduces waste, workload, and improves consistency.

Tools Used

MicrometerSOLIDWORKS3D printer

Highlights

  • One gauge supports three bottle types
  • Fast, consistent volume verification
