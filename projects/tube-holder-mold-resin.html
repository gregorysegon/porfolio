Tube Holder Mold for Resin Casting

Problem

Blood draw tubes roll around/off carts. Vendor tube holders were ~$60 each and the lab needed ~40 (too expensive).

Solution

Designed a 3D-printed negative to cast a silicone mold. The silicone mold is then used to cast resin tube holders at ~ $7 per part.

Tools Used

SOLIDWORKS3D printerTwo-part siliconeResin casting

Highlights

  • Reduced per-unit cost dramatically
  • Reusable silicone mold for repeated casts
  • Customization (e.g., name on underside)
