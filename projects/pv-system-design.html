PV System Design

Problem

Design a PV system for 354 K Street to offset annual usage of 9,406 kWh, within PG&E net energy metering limits.

Solution

Used solar availability data and roof geometry to determine optimal panel angles (south and east roof sections). Selected First Solar Series 7 TR1 cells and designed arrays on both roof faces to generate up to 110% of annual consumption given shading and roof-space constraints.

Tools Used

PG&E bills (2023)NREL Global Horizontal Solar Irradiance dataManufacturer datasheets (First Solar)

Highlights

  • Accounts for shading and roof limitations
  • Targets up to 110% annual generation (NEM constraint)
